Acme Corp — Q1 2025 Sales Report
===================================

Executive Summary
-----------------
Acme Corp recorded total revenue of $4,250,000 in Q1 2025, representing a 12% increase
compared to Q1 2024 ($3,794,643). Net profit for the quarter stood at $637,500 (15% margin).

Regional Breakdown
------------------
- North America : $2,100,000 (49.4% of total)
- Europe        : $1,062,500 (25.0% of total)
- Asia-Pacific  : $850,000   (20.0% of total)
- Rest of World : $237,500   ( 5.6% of total)

Top 5 Products by Revenue
--------------------------
1. Enterprise Suite Pro   — $1,350,000
2. DataSync Platform      — $980,000
3. CloudBridge Connector  — $760,000
4. Analytics Dashboard    — $620,000
5. SecureVault Backup     — $540,000

Customer Segments
-----------------
- Enterprise clients  : 42 accounts, avg deal size $68,000
- Mid-market clients  : 118 accounts, avg deal size $14,200
- SMB clients         : 530 accounts, avg deal size $2,830

Headcount
---------
Total employees as of 31-Mar-2025: 312
- Sales & Marketing : 87
- Engineering       : 124
- Operations        : 61
- G&A               : 40

Key Risks
---------
1. Supply chain disruptions may affect hardware-bundled product deliveries in Q2.
2. Increasing competition in the mid-market segment from two new entrants.
3. Foreign-exchange headwinds in the European market estimated at ~$85,000 impact.

Outlook
-------
Q2 2025 revenue guidance: $4,500,000 – $4,700,000.
New product launch (DataSync 2.0) expected in May 2025.
